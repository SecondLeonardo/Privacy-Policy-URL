Data Deletion Instructions for ST

Important: This page explains how users can request deletion of their data from ST app and associated Facebook data.

How to Delete Your ST Account and Data

Method 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Open the ST app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Scroll down to Account Management
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm deletion by entering your password
  6. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

Method 2: Email Request

  1. Send an email to: privacy@ST.app
  2. Include in your email:
    • Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
    • Your registered email address
    • Your ST username (if you remember it)
    • Reason for deletion (optional)
  3. We will process your request within 7 business days
  4. You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete

What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your ST account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Profile information (name, email, photo)
  • Skills and learning preferences
  • Chat messages and conversations
  • Match history
  • App usage data and analytics
  • Social media connection data (Facebook, Google)
  • Location data (if provided)
  • Account settings and preferences

Facebook Data Deletion

If you signed up using Facebook, deleting your ST account will also remove:

  • Data received from Facebook (profile info, email)
  • ST's access to your Facebook account
  • Any data shared between ST and Facebook
Note: To completely remove ST's access to your Facebook data, you should also:
  1. Go to Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites
  2. Find "ST" in the list
  3. Click "Remove" to revoke access

Data Retention

  • Immediate: Your account becomes inaccessible immediately
  • 30 days: All personal data is permanently deleted from our servers
  • Legal compliance: Some data may be retained longer if required by law
  • Anonymized data: Some usage statistics may be retained in anonymized form for analytics

Contact Information

For questions about data deletion or privacy concerns:

  • Email: privacy@ST.app
  • Response time: Within 7 business days
  • Privacy Policy: View our full Privacy Policy

User Rights

Under applicable privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Data portability (receive a copy of your data)

Last updated: December 17, 2024

This data deletion policy applies to all ST users and complies with Facebook's data deletion requirements.
